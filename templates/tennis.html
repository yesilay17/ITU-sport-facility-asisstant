Tennis courts are open during the week between 08:00 and 16:00.

Rules:

  • Note that you cannot make double reservation for same day.
  • You can make reservations up to a week later.
  • You should not enter the courts without asking the consultant and wearing clothes other than tennis clothing.
  • The name and surname of the players who will play in the reservation must be specified.
  • Reservation cancellations must be made at least 2 hours in advance, unless the cancellation is made, court fee (including members) is charged.
  • Waiting time on the courts is 10 minutes. At the end of this period, he/she can play waiting for the court.
  • Training activities on the courts are carried out under the supervision of branch teachers.
