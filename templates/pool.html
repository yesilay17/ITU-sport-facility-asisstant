Our pool has been opened to the service of ITU students, members and guests since June 18, 2007.

Rules:

  • People who will use the pool must definitely know how to swim.
  • If there is any health problem, a doctor should be consulted beforehand. If there is a health problem that may cause problems in the pool, the pool should never be used.
  • The pool should not be entered without a bonnet.
  • Only swimming equipment should be brought around the pool, and no slippers, daily clothes, shoes, bags, bags and similar items should enter the pool area.
  • Sea cannon, seabed, life buoy, armband etc. material should not be used in the pool.
  • The warnings of the lifeguards must be followed in terms of life safety.
  • You should not swim from the bottom of the pool while holding your breath, except for activities with special permission from the pool management.
  • You should not jump into the water in a way that disturbs the people around, run around the pool and make noise.
